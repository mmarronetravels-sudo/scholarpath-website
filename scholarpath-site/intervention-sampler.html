Why I built this

"What's actually in your library?"

It's the question that comes up in almost every Tier 2 conversation. School teams want to know what they'd be working with before they invest in another platform. So I pulled nine of the 71 pre-built interventions inside ScholarPath into a single PDF — three from Academic, three from Behavior, three from Social-Emotional. Names, monitoring cadences, decision rules. Plain language. No login, no email gate, no demo required.

It's a sampler, not the whole library. The intent is to make it easy to see whether ScholarPath is the right fit before any conversation has to happen.

What's inside · 9 interventions across 3 strands

Three strands. Three interventions each.

The Sampler mirrors the structure of the full library: each intervention sits inside one of three strands, each carries a tier (T2 or T3), and 17 across the library — including most of the ones featured here — ship with full plan templates.

What each card includes

Built like a quick reference, not a textbook.

Every intervention card in the Sampler runs the same fields, so flipping between them feels familiar. No fluff. The point is to make it easy to spot the ones you'd use, the ones you wouldn't, and the ones you'd want to learn more about.

One-line description

What the intervention is, in plain language, in under 140 characters.

Best fit when

The indication signal — the student-level pattern that suggests this intervention.

Monitoring cadence

How often progress is checked, and what kind of data you'd be looking at.

Decision rule

The trigger to fade, change course, or escalate — written so a team can act on it.

About this resource

What it is — and what it isn't.

The Sampler shows nine of the 71+ pre-built interventions inside ScholarPath, with the basics: the indication signal, the monitoring cadence, the decision rule. This is the menu.

What's not in the PDF: the goal-writing language, the materials checklists, the delivery scripts, the parent-portal letters, the fidelity tools, the data charts, the other sixty-plus interventions, and the unlimited custom-add capability. That's the kitchen — and it lives inside ScholarPath Intervention Management.

If you're tracking interventions across three platforms, a binder, and a spreadsheet, the gap this resource shows is the gap the product was built to close. Not a pitch. A reference point.
