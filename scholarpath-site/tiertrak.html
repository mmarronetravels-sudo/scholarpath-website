TierTrak supports RTI (Response to Intervention), PBIS (Positive Behavioral Interventions and Supports), and comprehensive MTSS frameworks.

How It Works

Get Started in Minutes

1

Import Your Students

Upload students via Excel/CSV or enter them manually. Assign grade levels, teachers, and initial tier placements.

2

Set Up Interventions

Create your intervention library and assign them to students. Set logging frequency (daily, weekly, bi-weekly) for each intervention.

3

Log Progress

Staff and parents log progress using a simple 1-10 scale with optional notes. Charts visualize growth over time.

4

Upload Documents

Store 504 Plans, IEPs, evaluations, and more. TierTrak automatically tracks expiration dates and alerts you when renewal is needed.

5

Review & Decide

Use progress data in MTSS team meetings to make data-driven decisions about tier transitions and intervention adjustments.
