Tier 2 Fidelity Check

Before you decide a student isn't responding, confirm the intervention was actually delivered as designed — and as often as planned. Sixteen quick checks across five areas.

Use this once per intervention, per student or group, every few weeks. Mark each item Yes, No, or N/A. Your fidelity score updates as you go.

Why fidelity first? A flat progress line tells you nothing if the intervention wasn't delivered the way it was designed. Rule out implementation before you change a student's plan — otherwise you may intensify supports a student never actually received.
