Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! Our Pilot plan lets you use the full product for a trimester or semester. The pilot fee is credited toward your annual plan if you continue.

How much do I save with a bundle?

Bundle pricing saves you $1,000-$1,500/year (or $2/student for Enterprise). You also get unified training and a single point of contact.

What's included in staff training?

Standard plans and above include live virtual training sessions for your team, plus recorded tutorials and documentation.

How do I import my existing data?

We accept Excel and CSV imports from any SIS. Our team will help you map your data and get up and running quickly.

Is my student data secure?

Absolutely. We're 100% FERPA compliant with SOC 2 certified infrastructure. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Can I cancel anytime?

Annual plans are billed yearly. You can cancel before your renewal date. Pilot plans are pay-as-you-go with no long-term commitment.
