Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Pilot?

The Pilot gives you full access to all features for 90 days. It's designed to let you evaluate the platform with real data before committing to an annual subscription. The pilot fee is credited toward your first year if you continue.

Can we switch tiers mid-year?

Absolutely! If your enrollment grows during the school year, we'll pro-rate the difference. We want the pricing to work for you as your school grows.

Is there a discount for multi-year contracts?

Yes! Contact us for special pricing on 2-year and 3-year agreements. Multi-year customers also receive priority access to new features.

What if we have multiple schools?

Our Enterprise tier includes multi-school support with district-level dashboards. Contact us for custom pricing based on your total enrollment across all schools.

Do you offer discounts for Title I schools?

We believe all schools deserve great tools. Contact us to discuss special pricing for Title I and high-need schools.
