The ScholarPath Leave Management Workflow

Simple, Compliant, Automatic

From the moment leave is entered to the moment it's reported, every step is handled — no spreadsheets, no manual calculations.

1
📥

Enter Leave

HR enters leave by date range or picks individual days with per-day hour inputs. Repeat entries with one click.

2
⚖️

Auto-Calculate

Entitlements are calculated automatically — flat 480 hrs for FMLA/OFLA, 560 hrs for birthing parents on PLO.

3
🔒

Enforce Protection

Protected leave periods prevent accidental edits. Concurrent leave flags automatically link FMLA, OFLA, and school-provided leave.

4
📊

Generate Reports

Export individual staff XLSX summaries or full district reports with one click — ready for HR and compliance.

Features

Everything Your HR Team Needs

⚖️

FMLA, OFLA & PLO Tracking

Track all protected leave types with correct entitlements, rolling periods, and concurrent leave flagging — built for Oregon schools.

📅

Intermittent Leave Calendar

Pick individual days with per-day hour inputs. Weekends are blocked, running totals update live, and each day saves as its own record.

🔄

Repeat Leave Entry

Duplicate any past entry with one click. All fields pre-fill — just update the dates and save. Perfect for recurring patterns.

📊

Real-Time HR Dashboard

See every staff member's leave balances, active protected leave cases, and low-balance alerts at a glance from one screen.

📥

One-Click XLSX Reports

Export individual leave summaries or full HR reports as Excel files — formatted, organized, and ready for your records or compliance review.

👁️

Weekly Leave View

Supervisors see who's out each week across their team with a clean, color-coded calendar grid — no digging through records.

🤱

Birthing Parent Flag

Automatically applies the 560-hour PLO entitlement for birthing parents. No manual overrides needed.

🔒

Protected Period Enforcement

Lock leave entries inside active FMLA/OFLA/PLO periods to prevent accidental edits and maintain accurate records.

👥

Role-Based Access

Staff see their own balances. Supervisors see their team. HR sees everyone. Admins manage everything. All with proper data security.
